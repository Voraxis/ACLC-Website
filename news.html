ACLC (AMA Computer Learning Center) News

News primarily revolves around enrollment promotions, discounts (like 45% off for late 2025/early 2026), new programs (Tech-Voc, IT, Business), campus activities, and success stories within the AMA Education System (AMAES) network, focusing on practical, IT-based education for high school grads and working professionals in the Philippines.

Enrollment Perks: Offers like significant tuition discounts (e.g., 45% off at end of 2025) for various programs (College, Grad Studies) are common.

Program Focus: Strong emphasis on IT-related courses (Computer Science, ICT, Animation) and Business/Accounting fields.

Flexibility: Options for virtual, on-campus, day, and night classes to suit different lifestyles.

Community & Events: Updates on campus life, cultural events (like global appreciation festivals), and achievements of alumni.

Where to Find ACLC News:

Official Website: aclc.edu.ph for general info and programs.

Facebook: Search for "ACLC College Official," "ACLC Malolos," etc., for real-time updates.

YouTube: For TV ads and highlights.

AMAES News Portal: For official system-wide announcements.
