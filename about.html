What is ACLC?

ACLC, or AMA Computer Learning Center, is a prominent Philippine educational network under the AMA Education System, known for providing IT-focused 2/4-year technical-vocational courses, short-term programs, and associate/bachelor's degrees, aiming to develop globally competitive knowledge workers with practical tech skills for local and international markets. They offer hands-on learning, industry-aligned curricula, online/blended options, and international affiliations to boost graduate employability in fields like IT, business, and hospitality.

ABOUT AMAES

The AMA Education System is a leading provider of IT-based education in the Philippines, and its flagship institution, AMA University and Colleges, is a member of this esteemed system. Founded by Dr. Amable R. Aguiluz V in 1980 and named after his father, Hon. Amable M. Aguiluz, AMA has become the largest network of colleges and universities in Asia, producing graduates who are highly competent in various technologies and applications needed in the industry.
